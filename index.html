What is sustainable investing?

Sustainable investing is about creating a better world by helping to solve the world's biggest challenges.

It's about better environmental, social and corporate governance.

It's about actively investing in companies offering products, services and solutions for the world's biggest challenges, like climate change and water scarcity.

Sustainable investing is becoming easier all the time and we are here to help get you started.

Why Invest in Sustainability?

#1 Impact

Sustainable investing is a great way to make a difference with your money.

There is compelling evidence to suggest that sustainable investing can generate good returns, especially in challenging times.

There has never been a better time to align your investments with your values.

#2 Growth

Sustainable investing is focused on long-term value creation.

Investing in positive environmental, social and governance attributes can be a sensible way to secure good return on your investment.

Companies solving global challenges, such as the climate crisis and water scarcity, are well placed to survive and thrive.

#3 Balance

Investing in sustainability can help to manage risk.

Recent research comparing thousands of traditional and sustainable investments over 14-years identified a lower downside risk in sustainable investments.
